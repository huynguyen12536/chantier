# Future Extension Points

## Tenant/company

CVL is single-tenant and has no `companies`, `company_id`, or company registry. The Unified Platform may later add an isolation boundary, tenant context, and company lifecycle only after a tenancy decision. Do not retroactively claim these are CVL rules.

## Pending Legacy Discovery

Reserve source provenance and adaptable capability mappings so a second legacy can be assessed against this pack. Any PLD object, rule, identity mapping, duplicate resolution, or migration requires its own evidence and Merge Decision.

## Super Admin

The brief’s Super Admin → Company provisioning flow (Flow H) is greenfield/deferred. It cannot be mapped to CVL `admin`, which is company-local. Future work may define platform roles, company provisioning, bootstrap administration, and tenant scoping after an explicit decision.

## Compatibility constraints

Extension points must not weaken CVL role, scope, timing, or integrity rules before a product decision. Frozen frontend names remain supported at the contract boundary.
